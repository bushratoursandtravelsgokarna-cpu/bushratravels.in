About Bushra Tours & Travels

Bushra Tours & Travels is your trusted taxi service provider in the holy town of Gokarna, Karnataka. We specialize in providing comfortable, reliable, and affordable transportation services for pilgrims visiting the sacred temples and tourists exploring the beautiful beaches of Gokarna.

Our experienced drivers have extensive knowledge of Gokarna and surrounding areas. Whether you need airport transfers, local sightseeing, or temple visits, we ensure a safe and pleasant journey. We operate 24/7 to serve our customers with dedication and professionalism.
